Welcome


Dosa is a popular South Indian delicacy that has captivated taste buds worldwide. This thin, crispy, and flavorful crepe is a staple in South Indian cuisine, known for its versatility and delicious taste. Made from a fermented batter of rice and urad dal (black gram), dosa is not just a dish; it's a culinary experience.
